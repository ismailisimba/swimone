About Us

Started in 1989 as a club to promote swimming safety, TALISS has grown to become one of the biggest and most competitive swimming clubs in Tanzania. We have performed and won numerous medals in national and international competitions, including The Olympics! TALISS also runs one of the oldest lifeguards training program in Tanzania. We have trained more than 10,000 lifeguards!

Coaches

Our coaches are the best in their field, coming from various clubs and the national team; Coach John Belela, Coach Alex Mwaipasi, Coach Siki, Coach Ezekiel, Coach Filex, Coach Said, Coach Crispin, Coach Martin

National and International Competitions

TALISS participates and hosts national and international competitions. We have attended and collected medals in CANA Zone 3 and The Olympics.

Membership, Training, and Practice

Members are required to be proficient to some degree. Members participate in regular friendly competitions and training sessions. As TALISS is invitied to competitions, members volunteer to represent the club and Tanzania. Contact us for details on how to become a member.

Lifeguards

TALISS offers professional lifeguard training. Started to promote swimming safety across Tanzania, we have trained over 10,000 lifeguards over our lifetime!
